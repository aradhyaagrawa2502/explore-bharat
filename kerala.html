🏨 Kerala Stay Guide

Best hotels split by Munnar, Thekkady, Alleppey & Kovalam

Best for tea gardens, mountain views & peaceful nature

Fragrant Nature Munnar

🌄 Luxury resort surrounded by hills and tea estates
🏊 Infinity pool + premium mountain views
💰 ₹10,000 – ₹20,000 per night

Amber Dale Luxury Hotel & Spa

🏔️ Comfortable luxury stay with valley views
📍 Great location for sightseeing
💰 ₹6,000 – ₹12,000 per night

Tea Valley Resort

🌿 Budget-friendly resort with scenic views
📸 Close to major attractions
💰 ₹3,000 – ₹6,000 per night

Best for wildlife, spice plantations & nature

Spice Village Resort

🌳 Eco-friendly luxury resort by CGH Earth
🏡 Traditional Kerala-style cottages
💰 ₹12,000 – ₹25,000 per night

Greenwoods Resort

🌿 Popular family-friendly resort
🌴 Surrounded by greenery
💰 ₹6,000 – ₹12,000 per night

Peppervine Hotel

🏨 Comfortable budget-mid range stay
📍 Close to Periyar attractions
💰 ₹3,000 – ₹6,000 per night



🚤 Days 6–7: Alleppey

Best for backwaters & houseboat experience

Luxury Houseboat Stay

🚤 Private houseboat with meals included
🌊 Most iconic Kerala experience
💰 ₹8,000 – ₹20,000 per night

Punnamada Resort

🌅 Beautiful backwater resort
🏊 Pool + lake views
💰 ₹6,000 – ₹15,000 per night

Lake Palace Resort

🌿 Budget-friendly waterfront resort
😌 Peaceful atmosphere
💰 ₹3,500 – ₹7,000 per night



🏖️ Day 8: Kovalam

Best for beaches, sunsets & relaxation

🌊 Premium beachfront luxury resort
🧘 Private beach vibes + spa
💰 ₹15,000 – ₹35,000 per night

The Leela Kovalam

🌅 Iconic cliff-top beach resort

Sagara Beach Resort
