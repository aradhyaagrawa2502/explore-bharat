🏖️ Family-friendly resort with pool

🍽️ Goa Food Guide

Must try, North Goa & South Goa food places

🌴🍽️ North Goa Food Places

🌿 Bean Me Up

🥗 Vegan & vegetarian café
🍲 Smoothie bowls + organic food
🌴 Calm jungle vibe near Vagator

🌿 Soul Fry (Veg Options Available)

🥗 Popular Goan café with veg dishes
🍛 Veg thali, curries, snacks
🌴 Cozy café vibe near Anjuna

🍤 Britto’s

🌊 Seafood restaurant at Baga Beach
🐟 Famous Goan dishes
🌅 Sunset dining spot

🌊🍽️ South Goa Food Places

🍗 Martin’s Corner

🍛 Authentic Goan cuisine
🐔 Chicken xacuti + seafood
🎶 Live music atmosphere

🌿 The Space Goa (Veg Café)

🥗 Vegetarian + vegan-friendly café
🍲 Healthy bowls, smoothies, salads
🌴 Peaceful café vibe in South Goa (Palolem area)

🐟 Fisherman’s Wharf

🌊 Riverside dining
🍤 Fresh seafood grills
💑 Romantic evening vibe
