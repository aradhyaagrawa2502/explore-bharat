🏨 Rajasthan Stay Guide

Best hotels split by Jaipur, Jodhpur & Jaisalmer

🏰 🌸 Jaipur (Pink City)

Best for forts, palaces, shopping & culture

Rambagh Palace

👑 Jaipur's most iconic luxury palace hotel
🏰 Royal experience with world-class hospitality
💰 ₹35,000 – ₹70,000+ per night

ITC Rajputana

⭐ Premium 5-star hotel in central Jaipur
🍽️ Great location + excellent dining
💰 ₹8,000 – ₹18,000 per night

Hotel Pearl Palace

🏨 Budget-friendly heritage hotel
🌸 Beautiful interiors + great value
💰 ₹2,000 – ₹4,500 per night



🔵 🏰 Jodhpur (Blue City)

Best for forts, heritage walks & local markets

Umaid Bhawan Palace

👑 One of the world's most luxurious palace hotels
🏰 Royal heritage experience
💰 ₹45,000 – ₹1,00,000+ per night

RAAS Jodhpur

🏨 Luxury boutique hotel near Mehrangarh Fort
🌄 Stunning fort views from rooms
💰 ₹15,000 – ₹35,000 per night

Krishna Prakash Heritage Haveli

🏡 Budget heritage stay near old city
🎭 Traditional Rajasthani atmosphere
💰 ₹2,000 – ₹5,000 per night

🍽️ Rajasthan Food Guide

Must try food places in Jaipur, Jodhpur & Jaisalmer

🏰🍽️ Jaipur Food Places

🌿 Laxmi Misthan Bhandar (LMB)

Famous vegetarian restaurant in Johari Bazaar
Rajasthani Thali, Dal Baati Churma, Ghewar

🌿 Tapri Central

Popular rooftop café with city views
Masala Chai, Paneer Sandwich, Pasta

🍗 Handi Restaurant

One of Jaipur's most famous non-veg restaurants
Laal Maas, Butter Chicken, Mutton Curry

🔵🍽️ Jodhpur Food Places

🌿 Gypsy Restaurant

Famous for authentic Rajasthani vegetarian food
Thali, Ker Sangri, Gatte Ki Sabzi

🌿 Janta Sweet Home

Iconic local food spot
Makhaniya Lassi, Kachori, Mirchi Vada

🍗 On The Rocks

Popular non-veg restaurant in Jodhpur
Rajasthani Mutton Curry, Kebabs

🏜️🍽️ Jaisalmer Food Places

🌿 The Trio Restaurant

Rooftop dining with fort views
Rajasthani Thali, Paneer Tikka

🌿 Café The Kaku

Cozy vegetarian café near Jaisalmer Fort
Pizza, Coffee, Indian Meals

🍗 Saffron Restaurant

Popular non-veg restaurant with fort views
Chicken Curry, Tandoori Chicken, Mutton Dishes
